AI Dungeon

AI Dungeon is a text-based adventure game that uses AI to generate responses to the player's actions. The game is unique in that it allows the player to take any action they can think of, and the AI will generate a response. This allows for a truly open-ended experience where the player can explore a vast world and interact with a wide variety of characters and objects.

The game is powered by OpenAI's GPT-3 language model, which is capable of generating human-like text based on a prompt. This allows the game to create rich and detailed responses to the player's actions, making for a highly immersive and engaging experience.
